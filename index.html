Read it and weep.

Everything written on this site is orginial content. The premiss should always be based on personal experience. A technique that has worked wonders for you and others. You should have something orginial to say about it. If there's already a better explaination available elsewhere, we link to it.

Conventions used in this document

Different sections are marked with an emoji according to the following categories:

  • :gem: = best practice
  • :green_apple: = convention
  • :gift_heart: = helpful tool

There's a significant difference between a best practice and a simple convention. Best practices are usually derived from experience and require some level of evidence to back them up. Conventions are often based on personal preference and are OK to break given some resonable argument.
